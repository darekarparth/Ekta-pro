My Nature, My Responsibility

As we depend on nature and nature depends on us in exchanging gases CO2 and O2. So its Individual’s responsibility as a global citizen, I too have the responsibilities to preserve nature & environment.


Objectives

To create awareness of Menstrual Hygiene among adolescent Girl and Working women for better health by installing Vending machine. To provide scientific solution at the source by installing environment friendly Destroyer to reduce Global growing problem of environment and hazardous garbage to dump saniwaste through toilet or through dust bean.

Reasons To Install In Colleges

  • To Encourage girls to go to schools due to backup of installed machines in schools.

  • Under the girl project, the girl students need to carry Rs. 5 coins which they can insert into the vending machines and get the sanitary pads.

  • After using the pads, the girl students can throw the used pads into the Destroyer installed at the school.

  • As per research found that a large number of girls leave school because of not having access to hygienic toilets and sanitary napkins during their menstrual periods and they prefer to stay home during those days, some of them eventually drop out of school after attaining puberty.
